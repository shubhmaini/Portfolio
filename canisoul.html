Canisoul

Canisoul – A comprehensive online resource dedicated to dogs, offering information on various breeds, health tips, care advice, training techniques, and gear recommendations. Designed to provide expert guidance for both new and experienced dog owners, Canisoul aims to strengthen the bond between humans and their canine companions through trusted, practical content.

Goals / Purpose

The goal of Canisoul is to create a centralized, user-friendly platform where dog lovers can find everything they need about dog ownership in one place. The purpose is to: Educate new dog owners about breed selection, care, and training. Provide reliable health and wellness guidance to prevent and manage common issues. Offer curated recommendations for dog gear and products. Build a community resource that promotes responsible dog ownership.

Tools & Technologies Used

1. Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Responsive Design)
2. Backend: WordPress (Custom Theme + CPT)
3. Plugins & Features: Custom Post Types, Taxonomies, Advanced Custom Fields, WP Forms
4. Design: Figma (UI/UX Wireframes, Mockups)
5. Deployment: Localhost development → Live server deployment

Process / Development Steps

1. Planning – Defined target audience and main categories (Dog Breeds, Dog Care, Training & Behavior, Gear & Products).
2. Design – Created wireframes and mockups in Figma, focusing on a clean, easy-to-navigate layout.
3. Development – Set up a WordPress site with a custom block theme. Created custom post types and taxonomies for structured content. Added category-specific templates for better readability.
4. Testing – Ensured responsiveness across devices and browsers; optimized for SEO and performance.
5. Deployment – Migrated from local environment to the live hosting server.

Challenges & Solutions

1. Challenge: Organizing large amounts of content without overwhelming users.
Solution: Used clear categories, search functionality, and filters for better navigation.
2. Challenge: Maintaining visual consistency across different post types.
Solution: Designed reusable templates and consistent typography/color styles.
3. Challenge: Optimizing loading speed for image-heavy content.
Solution: Used image compression tools and lazy loading.
